New York Cheesecake

Description

Silky New York Cheesecake with a crunchy crust

Ingrediants

  • 1 pound cream cheese, softened
  • 1 pound ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cups butter, melted and cooled
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups sour cream

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. Move oven rack to the middle of the oven.
  3. Beat the cream cheese, ricotta cheese, and sugar in a large mixing bowl with an electric mixer until combined.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time.
  5. Add the butter, flour, cornstarch, and vanilla extract.
  6. Mic until well combined.
  7. Fold in the sour cream and lemon juice.
  8. Pour the mixture into an ungreased 10-inch springform pan.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.
  10. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake in the oven for 2 more hours.
  11. Run a thin spatula around the edge of the cheesecake before springing open the pan to remove.
  12. Serve at room temperature or cold.
  13. Refrigerate leftovers.
